1. Brand Overview* MissionSpiceworx connects people, ideas, and businesses through clear communication, cultural intelligence, and modern digital solutions.* VisionTo be the trusted bridge for Japanese–Philippine collaborations, while providing world-class web and digital solutions for clients worldwide.* Core Services1. Interpreter ServicesJapanese–English–Filipino interpretation for corporate, industrial, and government contexts.2. Web Development & HostingSmart, secure, and user-friendly websites—available to a global market (not Japan-only).3. Workshops & Training* Cross-Cultural Business Training — helping Japanese and Philippine partners work seamlessly.* Leadership & Team Workshops — tailored to client needs, from communication to well-being.* (Future programs can be slotted here without needing to rewrite the whole service list.)* Core ValuesValueWhat it Looks LikeClaritySimplifying complexity in both language and techTrustLong-term relationships built on reliabilityCultural FluencyRespect for context and nuanceInnovationDigital-first, always evolvingEfficiencyNo fluff—solutions that work2. Target Audience* Japanese Corporates / SMEso Services: Training, interpreter, partnership facilitation.o What they need: Cultural trust, smooth communication, market understanding.* Philippine Organizationso Services: Training, interpreter, networking support.o What they need: Credibility with Japanese partners.* Global Businesses (esp. SMEs / boutique firms)o Services: Web development, hosting, and digital presence.o What they need: Cost-efficient, professional, secure web solutions.3. Brand Personality & Tone* Professional but approachable — not stiff, not “corporate-robot.”* Clear and concise — no jargon, direct language, always easy to understand.* Trusted guide — like a bridge between cultures and between tech & people.Tone: Warm, precise, confident.Example: “We make cross-cultural communication and digital presence simple, so you can focus on your business.”4. Visual Identity* Logoo Wordmark: SpiceWorx (green text)o Accent: Red “i” ? symbolizes chili pepper, “spice,” and a bold differentiator.* Color Palette (Chili Pepper Inspired)o Primary Green: Fresh, grounded, growth.o Accent Red: Energy, spice, action.o Secondary Neutral: Light grey or black for grounding.* Typographyo Clean sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans, Lato, Montserrat) for digital use.o Avoid overly decorative fonts—clarity first.* Graphic Styleo Simple, direct visuals.o Iconography to illustrate services (e.g., handshake for cultural workshops, chat bubble for interpretation, browser window for web dev).o Imagery = professional, real-world (people working, tech in action, cross-cultural moments).5. Messaging & Positioning* Tagline Optionso “Bridging Cultures. Building Solutions.”o “From Language to Digital—Clarity that Works.”o “Your Partner for Cross-Cultural and Digital Growth.”* Key Messageso “Spiceworx helps Japanese and Philippine businesses connect with clarity and confidence.”o “Our workshops and interpreter services build cultural fluency for stronger partnerships.”o “Our web solutions serve clients globally—modern, secure, and efficient.”* Elevator Pitch“Like a chili pepper—small but powerful—Spiceworx makes a big impact in the spaces where clarity matters most. We provide interpreter services that bridge Japanese, English, and Filipino for corporate and government settings, build smart and secure websites for a global market, and deliver workshops that strengthen cultural understanding, leadership, and team performance. At our core is cultural fluency and clear communication—helping businesses connect, grow, and succeed across borders.”6. Practical Use Guidelines* Use the green + red logo consistently (don’t stretch, skew, or recolor).* Stick to the chili palette—green primary, red accent. Use neutrals (white, grey, black) for balance.* Keep tone consistent: clear, approachable, reliable.* Adapt services messaging depending on audience:o Japan-facing ? emphasize cultural fluency and trust.o Global web dev ? emphasize reliability, security, and professionalism.SpiceWorx Consultancy, Inc.
