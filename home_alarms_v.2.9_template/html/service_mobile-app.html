Your Business Moves. Your App Should Too

Mobile isn't the future—it's now. Your customers check their phones 150+ times a day. If your business isn't in their pocket, you're invisible.

A mobile app does what a mobile website can't: it works offline, sends notifications that matter, accesses device features (camera, location, payments), and feels native to the phone it lives on.

For Japanese companies expanding into Southeast Asia, or Philippine organizations serving global markets, a mobile app is how you stay connected. It builds loyalty. It drives transactions. It's indispensable.

At SpiceWorx, we build mobile apps that users actually want to open.

What We Build

Native iOS & Android Apps

Performance that feels like magic

We build native apps for iPhone and Android using modern frameworks that deliver speed, reliability, and seamless integration with device capabilities. Your app feels like it belongs on the phone—because it does.

Example: A Japanese trading company needed a mobile app for their Philippine field teams to submit orders and photos in real time, even in areas with spotty connectivity. We built an offline-first app.

Cross-Platform Solutions

One codebase, two platforms, no compromise

For projects with tight timelines or budgets, we use cross-platform frameworks (Flutter, React Native) to build for iOS and Android simultaneously. You get to market faster without sacrificing quality.

Example: A startup needed an MVP in 3 months to validate their market. We used Flutter to launch on both iOS and Android together—cut the timeline in half.

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)

Mobile experience without the app store

Sometimes you don't need an app in the App Store. A Progressive Web App works like a native app—installs on the home screen, works offline, sends notifications—but lives on the web.

Example: A cross-cultural training company needed their course platform to work on phones. We built a PWA that installed like an app but updated automatically.

Built to Perform on Every Device

Native Development: Swift (iOS) & Kotlin (Android)

For apps that demand the best, we use Swift and Kotlin—the native languages built by Apple and Google. Your app has direct access to device capabilities, performs at peak efficiency, and feels perfectly at home on each platform.

Why it matters: Buttery-smooth animations. Instant response. Access to every device feature. Users notice and love it.

Cross-Platform: Flutter & React Native

When speed-to-market matters, we use Flutter or React Native to build for iOS and Android simultaneously. One team writes code once; both platforms benefit.

Why it matters: Faster development. Lower cost. Still high quality—especially for standard business apps.

Backend & Cloud Infrastructure

Your app needs a brain. We architect secure APIs, databases, and cloud infrastructure (AWS, Firebase, Google Cloud) that power your app, sync data across devices, and scale as you grow.

Why it matters: Real-time updates. Zero data loss. Millions of users handled gracefully.

Security & Offline-First Design

Mobile apps face unique challenges: unreliable networks, lost connections, stolen phones. We build with offline-first architecture—your app works without internet and syncs when connection returns. Data is encrypted. Sessions are secure.

Why it matters: Your users stay productive anywhere. Their data stays safe everywhere.
